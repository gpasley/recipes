Ingredients:
  - Turkey Wings (10) 
  - Olive oil 
  - pepper 
  - Italian seasoning 
  - seasoned salt  
  - aluminium foil

Directions:
  1. Gather your turkey wings and ingredients
  2. Place your wings in a baking dish. I remove the tips but its totally up to you.
  3. Pour about 1 cup of olive oil, and add 1 tablespoon of salt, pepper, seasoned salt & accent. Add more or less, season to        your taste.
  4. Preheat oven at 375
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45 mins. Then UNCOVER and BROIL for an additional 30 mins. This will allow the wings      to get a crispy, golden brown texture.
